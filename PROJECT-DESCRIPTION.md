# Product overview

Manoula is an **online marketplace** that connects **mothers** with **certified professionals** who specialize in **maternal wellness** and support.

## What users can do

- Discover certified maternal wellness professionals
- Filter by specialty and availability
- Review provider bios and reviews
- Message providers directly
- Book services
- Manage ongoing bookings

## What professionals get

A dashboard to:

- Manage their public profile
- Define services
- Manage availability and bookings

## Domains & specialties (examples)

The network spans areas such as **lactation**, **postpartum doula care**, **mental health / therapy**, **pelvic floor and physical therapy**, **sleep and infant care**, and **nutrition and wellness**—always framed around verified, credentialed support (see compliance below).

## Content & UX tone

- Prefer copy that is **calm**, **respectful**, and **inclusive** of pregnancy and postpartum experiences.
- Avoid sensational or fear-based language.

## Trust & compliance (product copy)

- Do not imply **medical diagnosis** or **outcome guarantees**.
- Reflect **verified / certified** language consistent with how the platform presents providers.
- Do **not** invent real certification bodies, licenses, or legal claims.
